Red Colony
=============

Red Colony (play here: [http://theplasm.github.com/red-colony](lol));


Controls:
---------

* WASD Motion
* Mouse camera rotation
* Left Click shooting
* 1 & 2: Orange and Blue
* 3: Place a voxel
* 4: Restore voxel
* 5: Destroy voxel
* R: Reset
